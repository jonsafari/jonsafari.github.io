Jon Dehdari



Research Interests

My general research interests include computational linguistics involving syntax and morphology. Specifically, statistical language modeling, unsupervised machine learning, machine translation, word clusters/vectors, syntactic parsing, speech recognition, morphological analysis & stemming, Persian NLP, Arabic NLP, efficient large-scale software for natural language processing. I'm also interested in language typology and universals.
