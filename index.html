Jon Dehdari





Research Interests

My general research interests include computational linguistics, syntax, and morphology. Specifically, language modeling, machine translation, unsupervised learning, syntactic parsing, word clusters/vectors, multilingual technologies, parallelization techniques, Persian NLP, Arabic NLP, morphological analysis, and speech recognition.
