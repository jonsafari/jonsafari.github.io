Language Technologies I teaches the theoretical foundation of modern computational linguistics.

Outline

  1. Formal models of language: possibilities
    HW1
  2. Statistical models of language: probabilities
    HW2
  3. Applications of language models
    HW3
  4. n-gram language models and smoothing
    HW4
  5. Parts of speech, word clusters, and class-based language models
    HW5
  6. Log-linear models
  7. Word vectors, and applications
    HW6
  8. Special guest lecture by Prof. Günter Neumann
  9. Probabilistic context-free grammars and syntactic language models
  10. Feedforward neural networks and autoencoders
  11. Recurrent neural network and their language models
  12. Character-based models of language
  13. Sequence-to-sequence models and neural machine translation
