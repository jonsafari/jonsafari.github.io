Tha Austrian , Andreas Widhoelzl , won this year 's New Year ski jump . 
Widhoelzl placed in front of the Japanese ski jumper Harada and Finnish ski jumper Ahonen . 
Sven Hannawald came in forth and was the best German to place . 
Two - time world champion , Martin Schmitt , who had won the preliminaries in Obersdorf , placed 11th in front of 30,000 spectators in the sold - out Olympic stadium .
Schmitt has now lost the lead in the overall placings in the German - Austrian tournament to Widhoelzl .
